## Geography
Eikengard is a large continent located within [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/The Root Beneath Roads\|The Root Beneath Roads]]. It spans just over 3 million square miles with its highest point being just over 30,000 feet tall at [[Mount Eikenhus\|Mount Eikenhus]] and its lowest being the [[Al Qahari Valley\|Al Qahari Valley]] located in [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Orakabad/Dhuria\|Dhuria]] reaching just over 2,000 feet deep. Eikengard spans many different ecosystems and Biomes from the Taiga in [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Harrofell/Harrofell\|Harrofell]] to the tropic rainforests in [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Del Auta/Del Auta\|Del Auta]] and [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Bayomori/Bayomori\|Bayomori]]. As stated, Eikengard is home to many differed locations and regions. In total it is home to 13 distinct cultural and ecologically different regions. 
## Ecosystem
Many factors influence the rich and dynamic biomes and landscapes. Hot wind blown by [[Balaga\|Balaga]], the South Wind, comes up from [[The Gnarl\|The Gnarl]] creating the dryness seen in [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Orakabad/Orakabad\|Orakabad]], [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Kurzar/Kurzar\|Kurzar]] and [[The Far Away Lands/The Root Beneath Roads/Eikengard/Eiken'sreach/Eiken'sreach\|Eiken'sreach]]. The winds in general have a great amount of effect on the ecosystem of Eikengard. On the other hand, in constant combat with the South Wind, [[Trianmann\|Trianmann]] blows winds and cool rains to wash away the heat of Balaga. To the north, [[Fruedlen\|Fruedlen]] blows his northerly wind from inside [[Moonendell\|Moonendell]] causing freezing and snow to coat the land. In the east, [[Heind\|Heind]] blows a mild consistent wind off the [[Sea of Leastad\|Sea of Leastad]]. This balance of power makes the tmperature difference, the seasons as some get more powerful and some get less in their battle of the skies earth and sea, and the basis for life on Eikengard. 

## Flora & Fauna
Eikegard is home to all kinds of animals, races of [[man\|man]] and deep secrets under the earth. Most of these were created as pets or birthed with the creation of the world and now roam freely throughout the lands.
